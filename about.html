Our Story

Founded on a Passion for Travel

Kalpvriksh Hospitality was born from a simple belief — that every journey should be transformative. Named after the mythical wish-fulfilling tree, we set out to make dream vacations accessible to every Indian traveller.

What started as a small travel consultancy in Gurugram has grown into a full-service hospitality brand, serving over 10,000 happy travellers across domestic and international destinations.

Today, we offer end-to-end travel solutions — from curated tour packages and flight bookings to visa assistance and forex services. Every itinerary is crafted with care, every traveller is treated like family.
