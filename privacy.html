Kalpvriksh Hospitality ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and travel services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you enquire about or book our services:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Travel preferences, destination, and number of travellers
  • Passport details (when required for visa or booking processing)
  • Payment information (processed securely through authorized payment gateways)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process travel enquiries and bookings
  • To communicate itinerary updates, visa status, and travel documents
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send promotional offers (only with your consent)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Payment transactions are encrypted and processed through PCI-compliant gateways.

Third-Party Sharing

We share necessary information with airlines, hotels, visa authorities, and insurance providers solely to fulfil your travel bookings. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at info@kalpvrikshhospitality.com.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.
